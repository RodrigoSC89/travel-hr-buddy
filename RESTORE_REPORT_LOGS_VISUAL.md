## ๐ฏ Key Design Decisions

1. **Cards over Table**: Better for mobile, easier to read
2. **Inline Filters**: Quick access, no modal needed
3. **Auto-update**: Filters trigger immediate refresh
4. **Visual Status**: Colors + emojis for quick scanning
5. **Expandable Errors**: Keep UI clean, show details when needed
6. **CSV Export**: Standard format, easy to analyze
7. **Toast Feedback**: Non-intrusive notifications
