## ğŸ¨ Visual Design System

### Colors
- **Background:** `white` (default)
- **Reply background:** `bg-gray-50`
- **Border (replies):** `border-gray-200` (2px)
- **Text:** `text-base` (comments), `text-sm` (replies)
- **Muted text:** `text-muted-foreground` (timestamps, authors)
- **Button hover:** `hover:bg-primary/10`

### Spacing
- **Comment cards:** `space-y-4` (16px gap)
- **Reply indent:** `ml-6 pl-4` (24px left margin + 16px padding)
- **Reply gaps:** `space-y-3` (12px gap)
- **Reaction buttons:** `gap-2` (8px gap)

### Typography
- **Comment text:** `text-base` (16px)
- **Reply text:** `text-sm` (14px)
- **Timestamps:** `text-xs text-muted-foreground` (12px)
- **Section headers:** `text-sm font-medium` (14px, bold)
